My Hometown

I come from Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam!

Friendly!

People in Vietnam is known to be friendly, and I can proudly say that people in Hanoi is (probably) the friendliest in my country!

Foods and Drinks

Foods in Hanoi ars simply amazing. Here you can find and eat the famous Pho and Banh Mi. You can also find a variety of bún (vermicelli) and miến (thinner vermicelli). Drinks here are great as well, especially coffee. One sip of that and you will be awake for hours :)

Tourist Attractions

From historical sites to the old streets, Hanoi have always been an enjoyable place to visit. 
You can consider visit these tourist attractions:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake
  • Thang Long Imperial Citadel
  • Temple of Literature
  • The Old Quarter
  • And many more!
